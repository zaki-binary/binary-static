Regulatory Information

Binary.com is a licensed and regulated trading platform that serves over 1,000,000 customers globally. We hold multiple licenses to comply with regulations around the world. Since 1999, we've served our clients with a constant and unwavering commitment to integrity and reliability. We always hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, in addition to our regulatory requirements.

Deriv Investments (Europe) Limited

In the EU, financial products are offered by Deriv Investments (Europe) Limited, W Business Centre, Level 3, Triq Dun Karm, Birkirkara, BKR 9033, Malta, licensed and regulated as a Category 3 Investment Services provider by the Malta Financial Services Authority (licence no. IS/70156).

Deriv Investments (Europe) Limited, W Business Centre, Level 3, Triq Dun Karm, Birkirkara BKR 9033, Malta, is licensed in Malta and authorised by the Malta Financial Services Authority under the Investments Services Act to provide investment services in the European Union.

It is also authorised and subject to limited regulation by the Financial Conduct Authority in the UK. Details about the extent of our authorisation and regulation by the Financial Conduct Authority are available from us on request.

European Union residents who wish to trade investment products will have their accounts opened with Deriv Investments (Europe) Limited.
